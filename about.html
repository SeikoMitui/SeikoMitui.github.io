About

Seiko Mitui

Seiko Mitui grew up in the Fukui prefecture in japan. She has worked several jobs which include car scrap selling and working at a cafe, which she owned. Mitui currently owns a BBQ restaurant in Ishikawa prefecture. Mitui is interested in social issues, such as descrimination, which influence her art.
Seiko Mitui discovered painting nearly twenty years ago and began studying under Shunroku Hayashi. Since then, her passion for painting has grown. She continues to paint in her home in the Ishikawa prefecture.

三井聖子

三井聖子は石川県に在住。

第三の人生を歩みたく、今を生きている。

楽しく思いのまま描きたく、そして一生懸命働いています。

スクラップ、バー、居酒屋、その中で少し明かりが見えてきた。その明かりに向かって歩きたいです。

深くて広い差別と偏見が続く中、少しづつ消えていく事と個々がお互いに信じ合える様願います。

敬愛する林 俊六先生に師事。
